Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Butter
  • American cheese slices

Steps

  1. Take 2 pieces of bread and butter both sides well.
  2. Add 1 slice of American cheese to bread and close the sandwich.
  3. Bring a pan to a medium heat.
  4. When pan is warm, add butter.
  5. Add sandwich to the pan, compress it down with your spatula.
  6. Flip and cook until brown.
  7. Serve and enjoy.
