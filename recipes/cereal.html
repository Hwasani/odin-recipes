Farina

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups water
  • cinnamon powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups farina

Steps

  1. Heat a medium-sized pot on medium-low heat.
  2. Add the milk and water, let simmer.
  3. When it's come to a simmer, add farina.
  4. Stir frequently and let cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Pour into bowls and add cinnamon.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
