Homemade Smashburgers

Ingredients

  • 75/25 Ground Beef
  • Sliced American Cheese
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Measure out patties to 125 grams, add onion powder and garlic powder, 3 grams each.
  2. Roll the measured patties into a ball
  3. With a pinch of salt, salt the top of both patties
  4. Preheat griddle/pan to 150 degrees Celsius or 300 degrees Fahrenheit
  5. Toast Hamburger Buns (You can use butter if you'd like.)
  6. Place patties on griddle/pan and let them sit for 10 seconds each
  7. Take a spatula and press down hard on the patties until they're flat
  8. After 1 minute, flip the burgers and add a pinch of salt
  9. Place sliced cheese on the patties and take them off the heat and into the buns
  10. Serve and enjoy
